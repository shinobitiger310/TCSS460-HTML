Whales

Includes information on Blue Whales and Sperm Whales

Blue Whale

Blue whales can reach ~30 m (100 ft) and up to ~200 tons. They mainly eat krill and, despite their size, are gentle giants.

Sperm Whale

Sperm whales are deep divers (900+ m) that hunt squid and live in social pods. Males can reach ~18 m (60 ft).
